About Us

Street Furpaws is a dedicated non-governmental organization situated in the heart of the City of Sta. Rosa, nestled within the picturesque province of Laguna in the Philippines. Our primary mission revolves around the compassionate rescue and care of cats and dogs that find themselves in dire need of assistance. With unwavering commitment, we provide a safe haven for these vulnerable animals, offering them a second chance at life filled with love and care.

Feeding Program

On a monthly basis, Street Furpaws initiates a heartwarming stray feeding program, an endeavor that demonstrates our dedication to the welfare of the community's stray animals. Through this program, we endeavor to alleviate the suffering of these creatures by ensuring they have access to nourishing meals and clean water.
